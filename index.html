OUR STORY

Sin Gin Distillery is an award winning Australian distillery that began as a conversation between Kate & Mark at The Murray Hotel in West Perth, Western Australia in 2016 with the first product release in August 2017. In early 2018 Sin Gin entered the Australian Distilled Spirits Awards in Melbourne and won 1 Gold, 3 Silver & 2 Bronze medals; an outstanding performance for this nano distillery in it’s first year and encouraged us to grow knowing our product was excellent.

Four Ways To Enjoy Gin With Friends

Gin Tasting Parties - We Come To You

We have every type of gin party for your event whether large or small, corporate or birthday, at home or at the office. Our packages includes bottles of Sin Gin plus a Sin Gin professional who will take your guests through a one hour tasting to kick your party off before leaving you to enjoy your favourite gins at your leisure.

The Sin Gin Tasting DIY Party Pack

This gin tasting pack gives you everything you need to hold a tasting in the comfort of your own home or office with enough for tastings and to serve extra G&Ts. You get the full set of 7 Deadly Sin Gins 200ml bottles, plus a Hosting Pack, including tasting notes, ideas for how to run your tasting party…even recipe ideas for canapés to serve.

Gin Tasting @ The Distillery

Come on your own or gather your friends and come to a Sin Gin Tasting at The Distillery in the Swan Valley where the distillery staff will give tastings of a range of our gins including the Seven Deadly Sin Gins & Perth Pink Gin. Fundraising for a sporting group or charity?

A Gin Party is a great way to get people together for a good cause. We recommend charging a price per guest that covers the party costs and then by adding simple elements such as finger food, a door prize, silent auction, entertainment, even a ‘Seven Deadly Sins’ theme.
